> [!Definition]+ Def: [[Pages/Principal Normal and Binormal|Principal Normal and Binormal]]
> The **principal normal** along a [[Pages/Norm of a Curve|unit-speed]] [[Pages/Biregular Curves#^624e81|biregular]] curve $x(s)$ is
> $$N(s) = \frac{T'(s)}{\lvert T'(s) \rvert } = \frac{T'(s)}{\kappa}$$
> The **binormal** vector field along $x(s)$ is
> $$B = T \times N$$
